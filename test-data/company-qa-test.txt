## Products and Services

### 1. CloudAI Platform
Our flagship product is CloudAI Platform, an enterprise AI solution that helps businesses automate workflows and make data-driven decisions. The platform features:
- Natural Language Processing (NLP) for document analysis
- Predictive analytics and forecasting
- Automated report generation
- Real-time data visualization
- Integration with 50+ enterprise tools (Salesforce, SAP, Oracle, etc.)

**Pricing:** Starting at $5,000/month for small businesses, up to $50,000/month for enterprise clients

### 2. DataInsight Analytics
A specialized analytics tool for e-commerce businesses that provides:
- Customer behavior analysis
- Sales forecasting with 95% accuracy
- Inventory optimization recommendations
- Marketing campaign performance tracking
- Competitor analysis

**Pricing:** $2,500/month (Standard), $7,500/month (Premium)

### 3. SecureDoc AI
Document management system with AI-powered security features:
- Automatic document classification
- Sensitive information detection and redaction
- Compliance monitoring (GDPR, HIPAA, SOC 2)
- Version control and audit trails
- Encrypted storage with AES-256

**Pricing:** $1,500/month + $10 per user

## Frequently Asked Questions

### For Prospective Employees

**Q: What's the interview process like?**
A: Our process typically takes 2-3 weeks:
1. Initial phone screen (30 minutes) with recruiter
2. Technical assessment (take-home project, 3-4 hours)
3. Virtual onsite (4 hours): coding interview, system design, behavioral interview, and team fit discussion
4. Final interview with hiring manager or executive team
5. Offer decision within 48 hours

**Q: Do you sponsor work visas?**
A: Yes! We sponsor H-1B, TN, and other work visas for qualified candidates. We also support green card applications after 1 year of employment.

**Q: What's the promotion timeline?**
A: We conduct performance reviews twice a year (June and December). Promotions are merit-based, with typical timelines:
- Junior to Mid-level: 18-24 months
- Mid-level to Senior: 2-3 years
- Senior to Staff/Principal: 3-4 years

**Q: Can I work fully remote?**
A: Yes! About 30% of our employees work fully remote. We provide home office setup, co-working allowance, and fly remote employees to HQ once per quarter for team events.

**Q: What's the dress code?**
A: Casual. Wear whatever makes you comfortable and productive. Most employees wear jeans and t-shirts.

### For Customers

**Q: What security certifications do you have?**
A: We are SOC 2 Type II certified, GDPR compliant, and HIPAA compliant. We undergo annual third-party security audits.

**Q: What's your SLA uptime guarantee?**
A: We guarantee 99.9% uptime for all our products. If we fall below this, customers receive service credits:
- 99.0% - 99.9%: 10% credit
- 95.0% - 99.0%: 25% credit
- Below 95%: 50% credit

**Q: Do you offer custom integrations?**
A: Yes! Our Enterprise plan includes up to 10 custom integrations per year at no additional cost. Additional integrations are $5,000 each.

**Q: What's your data retention policy?**
A: We retain customer data for the duration of the contract plus 90 days. After that, data is permanently deleted unless customers request extended retention (available for additional fee).

**Q: Can we host on-premise?**
A: Yes! Our Enterprise plan includes an on-premise deployment option. This requires a minimum contract of $100,000/year.

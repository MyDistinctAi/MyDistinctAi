WORK SCHEDULE AND ATTENDANCE
================================================================================

Standard Work Hours:
- Monday to Friday: 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM
- Lunch Break: 1 hour (flexible timing)
- Total Weekly Hours: 40 hours

Flexible Work Options:
- Remote Work: Available 2 days per week
- Flexible Start Time: Between 8:00 AM - 10:00 AM
- Core Hours: 10:00 AM - 3:00 PM (must be available)

Attendance Policy:
- Employees must notify their manager by 8:30 AM if unable to attend
- More than 3 unexcused absences in 90 days may result in disciplinary action
- Tardiness (arriving after 9:15 AM) is tracked and reviewed quarterly

================================================================================
PAID TIME OFF (PTO)
================================================================================

Vacation Days:
- Years 1-3: 15 days per year
- Years 4-7: 20 days per year
- Years 8+: 25 days per year

Sick Leave:
- 10 days per year
- Can be used for personal illness or family care
- Doctor's note required for absences exceeding 3 consecutive days

Holidays (12 paid holidays):
- New Year's Day
- Martin Luther King Jr. Day
- Presidents' Day
- Memorial Day
- Independence Day
- Labor Day
- Thanksgiving (2 days)
- Christmas Eve
- Christmas Day
- New Year's Eve
- 2 Floating Holidays (employee's choice)

Parental Leave:
- Birth Parent: 16 weeks paid leave
- Non-Birth Parent: 8 weeks paid leave
- Adoption: 12 weeks paid leave

COMPENSATION AND BENEFITS
================================================================================

Salary Structure:
- Entry Level: $55,000 - $75,000
- Mid Level: $75,000 - $110,000
- Senior Level: $110,000 - $160,000
- Management: $140,000 - $220,000

Annual Reviews:
- Performance reviews conducted every April
- Salary adjustments based on performance and market rates
- Average merit increase: 3-7%

Bonus Structure:
- Annual performance bonus: Up to 15% of base salary
- Paid in December based on company and individual performance
- Spot bonuses available for exceptional contributions

Health Insurance:
- Medical: 90% employer-paid premium
- Dental: 100% employer-paid premium
- Vision: 100% employer-paid premium
- Coverage starts on first day of employment

Retirement Benefits:
- 401(k) plan with 5% company match
- Immediate vesting
- Financial planning services provided

Additional Benefits:
- Life Insurance: 2x annual salary (employer-paid)
- Disability Insurance: Short-term and long-term coverage
- Employee Assistance Program (EAP)
- Gym Membership: $50/month reimbursement
- Professional Development: $2,000 annual budget
- Home Office Stipend: $500 annually for remote workers

PROFESSIONAL DEVELOPMENT

Training Programs:
- New Employee Orientation: 2-week comprehensive program
- Technical Skills Training: Quarterly workshops
- Leadership Development: Annual program for managers
- Lunch & Learn Sessions: Weekly knowledge sharing

Education Assistance:
- Tuition Reimbursement: Up to $5,000 per year
- Certification Programs: Fully covered if job-related
- Conference Attendance: 2 conferences per year approved

Career Growth:
- Internal Job Postings: Employees get first consideration
- Mentorship Program: Available to all employees
- Quarterly Career Development Discussions with managers

================================================================================
WORKPLACE POLICIES
================================================================================

Dress Code:
- Business Casual: Monday through Thursday
- Casual Friday: Jeans and company t-shirts allowed
- Client Meetings: Business professional attire required
- Remote Work: Professional attire for video calls

Technology Use:
- Company laptop and phone provided
- Personal use of company devices allowed within reason
- Social media use during work hours should be limited
- All company data must be encrypted and secured

Confidentiality:
- All employees must sign NDA upon hire
- Client information is strictly confidential
- Sharing proprietary information is grounds for termination
- Data breaches must be reported immediately to IT Security

Harassment and Discrimination:
- Zero tolerance policy for harassment of any kind
- All complaints investigated promptly and confidentially
- Retaliation against complainants is prohibited
- Annual harassment prevention training required

Safety and Security:
- Building access requires badge at all times
- Visitors must sign in and be escorted
- Emergency exits clearly marked on each floor
- Fire drills conducted quarterly

================================================================================
REMOTE WORK POLICY
================================================================================

Eligibility:
- Must have been with company for 3+ months
- Performance rating of "Meets Expectations" or higher
- Role must be suitable for remote work

Requirements:
- Dedicated workspace with reliable internet (minimum 50 Mbps)
- Available during core business hours (10 AM - 3 PM)
- Respond to messages within 30 minutes during work hours
- Attend all required video meetings with camera on

Equipment:
- Company provides: Laptop, monitor, keyboard, mouse
- Employee provides: Desk, chair, internet connection
- IT support available for company equipment only

================================================================================
EXPENSE REIMBURSEMENT
================================================================================

Business Expenses:
- Travel: Airfare, hotel, ground transportation
- Meals: Up to $75 per day while traveling
- Client Entertainment: Pre-approval required over $100
- Office Supplies: Up to $50/month without approval

Submission Process:
- Submit expenses within 30 days of incurrence
- Receipts required for all expenses over $25
- Reimbursement processed within 2 weeks
- Use Expensify app for all submissions

Mileage Reimbursement:
- Personal vehicle use: $0.67 per mile (IRS rate)
- Must be for business purposes only
- Commuting to regular office not reimbursable

================================================================================
TERMINATION AND RESIGNATION
================================================================================

Resignation:
- Two weeks notice required for non-management
- Four weeks notice required for management positions
- Exit interview conducted by HR
- Final paycheck includes unused PTO

Company-Initiated Termination:
- At-will employment (can be terminated at any time)
- Severance package based on tenure and circumstances
- COBRA information provided for health insurance continuation
- Outplacement services available for layoffs

Return of Property:
- All company equipment must be returned
- Access badges, keys, and credit cards
- Final paycheck withheld until all items returned

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
--------------------------
Q: Can I work from a different time zone?
A: Yes, as long as you have 4 hours of overlap with core hours (11 AM - 3 PM Central Time).

Q: How do I request time off?
A: Post in #time-off Slack channel and add to the shared calendar. No approval needed for less than 2 weeks.

Q: What's the dress code?
A: Wear what makes you comfortable and productive. Business casual for client meetings.

Q: Can I bring my dog to the office?
A: Yes! We're dog-friendly. Just make sure they're well-behaved and up to date on vaccinations.

Q: How often do we get raises?
A: Annual merit increases based on performance. Promotions come with salary adjustments.

Q: What's the interview process like?
A: Phone screen → Technical/Skills assessment → Team fit interview → Founder interview → Offer

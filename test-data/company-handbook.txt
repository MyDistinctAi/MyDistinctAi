==============================================
VACATION POLICY
==============================================

Full-time employees receive:
- Year 1-2: 15 days paid vacation
- Year 3-5: 20 days paid vacation  
- Year 6+: 25 days paid vacation

Additional Benefits:
- 10 paid sick days per year
- 12 paid holidays
- 5 personal days
- Unlimited unpaid time off (with manager approval)

Vacation Request Process:
1. Submit request at least 2 weeks in advance
2. Get manager approval via email
3. Update team calendar
4. Set out-of-office auto-reply

==============================================
REMOTE WORK POLICY
==============================================

Eligibility:
- Must be employed for at least 6 months
- Performance rating of "Meets Expectations" or higher
- Role must be suitable for remote work

Remote Work Options:
- Hybrid: 2-3 days in office, 2-3 days remote
- Full Remote: Available for specific roles only
- Flexible Hours: Core hours 10am-3pm, flexible outside that

Equipment Provided:
- Laptop (MacBook Pro or Dell XPS)
- Monitor (27-inch 4K)
- Keyboard and mouse
- $500 home office stipend

Internet Reimbursement:
- Up to $75/month for home internet
- Must provide monthly invoice
- Submit via expense system by 5th of each month

==============================================
HEALTH INSURANCE
==============================================

Coverage Starts: First day of employment

Plan Options:
1. Gold Plan
   - Premium: $150/month (employee portion)
   - Deductible: $500
   - Out-of-pocket max: $2,000
   - Coverage: 90% after deductible

2. Silver Plan
   - Premium: $75/month (employee portion)
   - Deductible: $1,500
   - Out-of-pocket max: $5,000
   - Coverage: 80% after deductible

3. Bronze Plan
   - Premium: $25/month (employee portion)
   - Deductible: $3,000
   - Out-of-pocket max: $7,500
   - Coverage: 70% after deductible

Dental & Vision:
- Included in all plans at no extra cost
- Dental: 2 cleanings/year, 80% coverage for procedures
- Vision: Annual eye exam, $200 towards glasses/contacts

==============================================
401(K) RETIREMENT PLAN
==============================================

Company Match:
- 100% match up to 6% of salary
- Vesting schedule: 20% per year (fully vested after 5 years)
- Immediate enrollment available

Investment Options:
- Target date funds
- Index funds (S&P 500, Total Market)
- Bond funds
- International funds

Contribution Limits (2024):
- Employee: Up to $23,000/year
- Catch-up (age 50+): Additional $7,500/year

==============================================
PROFESSIONAL DEVELOPMENT
==============================================

Annual Budget: $2,000 per employee

Eligible Expenses:
- Online courses (Coursera, Udemy, LinkedIn Learning)
- Conference attendance
- Professional certifications
- Technical books and subscriptions
- Workshops and seminars

Approval Process:
1. Discuss with manager
2. Submit request via Learning Portal
3. Get VP approval for expenses over $1,000
4. Complete within 12 months

Popular Certifications:
- AWS Certified Solutions Architect: $300
- PMP (Project Management): $555
- Certified Scrum Master: $1,000
- Google Cloud Professional: $200

==============================================
PERFORMANCE REVIEWS
==============================================

Schedule:
- Quarterly check-ins (informal)
- Annual performance review (formal)
- Mid-year review (formal)

Rating Scale:
1. Exceeds Expectations (Top 10%)
2. Meets Expectations (70%)
3. Needs Improvement (15%)
4. Unsatisfactory (5%)

Compensation Impact:
- Exceeds: 8-12% raise + bonus
- Meets: 3-5% raise + bonus
- Needs Improvement: 0-2% raise, no bonus
- Unsatisfactory: Performance improvement plan

==============================================
PARENTAL LEAVE
==============================================

Birth Parent:
- 16 weeks paid leave
- Can be taken within 12 months of birth
- Full salary continuation

Non-Birth Parent:
- 8 weeks paid leave
- Can be taken within 12 months of birth/adoption
- Full salary continuation

Adoption Leave:
- 12 weeks paid leave
- Applies to both parents
- Additional $5,000 adoption assistance

Return to Work:
- Flexible return schedule available
- Gradual ramp-up option (part-time for first month)
- Lactation rooms available in all offices

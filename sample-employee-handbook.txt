EMPLOYEE HANDBOOK
ACME Corporation - 2025 Edition

WELCOME TO ACME!
We're thrilled to have you join our team. This handbook outlines our company culture, policies, and expectations.

CODE OF CONDUCT
1. Respect & Professionalism
   - Treat all colleagues with dignity and respect
   - Maintain professional behavior in all interactions
   - Zero tolerance for harassment or discrimination
   - Report concerns to HR immediately

2. Confidentiality
   - Protect company and client information
   - Do not share proprietary data
   - Sign NDA upon hire
   - Return all materials upon departure

3. Conflict of Interest
   - Disclose any potential conflicts
   - No outside work that competes with ACME
   - Approval required for board positions
   - Report gifts over $50 value

DRESS CODE
- Monday-Thursday: Business Casual
  * Collared shirts, blouses, dress pants
  * No jeans, sneakers, or t-shirts
  * Professional appearance required

- Friday: Casual
  * Jeans and sneakers allowed
  * Still maintain neat appearance
  * No offensive graphics or slogans

- Client Meetings: Business Formal
  * Suits or equivalent
  * Professional footwear
  * Conservative colors

WORKPLACE BEHAVIOR
- Punctuality: Arrive on time for meetings
- Communication: Respond to emails within 24 hours
- Collaboration: Support team members
- Innovation: Share ideas and improvements
- Accountability: Own your work and mistakes

PERFORMANCE EXPECTATIONS
- Quality: Deliver high-quality work
- Deadlines: Meet project timelines
- Initiative: Proactively solve problems
- Learning: Continuously develop skills
- Feedback: Accept and act on constructive criticism

TRAINING & DEVELOPMENT
- Onboarding Program: 2-week comprehensive training
- Quarterly Workshops: Skill development sessions
- Leadership Training: For managers and aspiring leaders
- Certifications: Company supports relevant certifications
- Conferences: Attend 1-2 industry events per year

COMMUNICATION CHANNELS
- Email: Primary for formal communication
- Slack: Quick questions and team chat
- Zoom: Virtual meetings and remote collaboration
- In-Person: Preferred for sensitive topics
- Town Halls: Monthly all-hands meetings

TECHNOLOGY USE
- Equipment: Laptop, monitor, phone provided
- Software: Licensed tools only
- Security: Use VPN for remote access
- Personal Use: Limited personal use acceptable
- Social Media: Follow company guidelines

HEALTH & SAFETY
- Ergonomics: Request ergonomic assessment
- Breaks: Take regular breaks (every 2 hours)
- Mental Health: EAP services available
- First Aid: Kits on every floor
- Emergency: Know evacuation procedures

EQUAL OPPORTUNITY
ACME is an equal opportunity employer. We do not discriminate based on race, color, religion, sex, national origin, age, disability, or any other protected characteristic.

QUESTIONS?

ACME CORPORATION - COMPANY POLICIES

WORK HOURS & SCHEDULE
- Standard Hours: Monday-Friday, 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM
- Lunch Break: 1 hour (12:00 PM - 1:00 PM)
- Flexible Hours: Available for employees with 2+ years tenure
- Core Hours: All employees must be available 10:00 AM - 3:00 PM

REMOTE WORK POLICY
- Hybrid Schedule: 2 days remote, 3 days in-office
- Required In-Office Days: Tuesday and Thursday
- Full Remote: Available with manager approval for special circumstances
- Equipment: Company provides laptop and monitor for remote work

VACATION & TIME OFF
- Year 1-2: 15 days paid vacation
- Year 3-5: 20 days paid vacation
- Year 6+: 25 days paid vacation
- Sick Days: 10 days per year (separate from vacation)
- Personal Days: 3 days per year
- Advance Notice: Request vacation at least 2 weeks in advance

BENEFITS PACKAGE
- Health Insurance: Medical, Dental, Vision (100% premium covered)
- 401(k) Retirement: 5% company match
- Life Insurance: 2x annual salary
- Disability Insurance: Short-term and long-term
- Professional Development: $2,000 annual budget per employee
- Gym Membership: $50/month reimbursement

PERFORMANCE & COMPENSATION
- Annual Reviews: Conducted every December
- Mid-Year Check-ins: June performance discussions
- Merit Increases: Based on performance, typically 3-7%
- Bonuses: Annual performance bonus (10-20% of salary)
- Promotion Cycle: Twice yearly (January and July)

WORKPLACE SAFETY GUIDELINES
ACME Corporation Safety Department

EMERGENCY PROCEDURES

Fire Emergency:
1. Sound the alarm immediately
2. Evacuate via nearest stairwell (DO NOT use elevators)
3. Close doors behind you
4. Proceed to assembly point: North parking lot
5. Do not re-enter until cleared by fire department
6. Emergency Contact: 911 or Security ext. 5555

Medical Emergency:
1. Call 911 immediately
2. Notify Security ext. 5555
3. Do not move injured person unless in immediate danger
4. Trained first responders available on each floor
5. AED devices located in main lobby and each floor
6. First aid kits in break rooms

Severe Weather:
1. Monitor weather alerts
2. Shelter in interior rooms away from windows
3. Tornado: Lowest floor, interior room
4. Earthquake: Drop, Cover, Hold On
5. Follow instructions from emergency coordinators

WORKPLACE SAFETY RULES

General Safety:
- Keep walkways and exits clear at all times
- Report spills immediately to facilities
- Use handrails on stairs
- No running in hallways
- Proper footwear required (no flip-flops)
- Report hazards to supervisor immediately

Ergonomics:
- Adjust chair height: feet flat on floor
- Monitor at eye level, arm's length away
- Keyboard and mouse at elbow height
- Take micro-breaks every 30 minutes
- Request ergonomic assessment if needed
- Use document holder for frequent reference

Lifting & Moving:
- Bend knees, not back
- Keep load close to body
- Ask for help with heavy items (over 25 lbs)
- Use carts or dollies when available
- Never carry items that block your view
- Team lift for awkward or heavy objects

INCIDENT REPORTING

When to Report:
- Any injury, no matter how minor
- Near-miss incidents
- Unsafe conditions or equipment
- Harassment or threatening behavior
- Security concerns

How to Report:
1. Immediate: Notify supervisor
2. Within 24 hours: Complete incident report form
3. Serious incidents: Also notify HR and Security
4. Anonymous: Use safety hotline ext. 5999
5. Follow-up: Participate in investigation if requested

PERSONAL PROTECTIVE EQUIPMENT (PPE)

When Required:
- Lab work: Safety glasses, gloves, lab coat
- Warehouse: Steel-toe boots, hard hat, safety vest
- Maintenance: Appropriate PPE per task
- Visitors: Must follow same PPE requirements

Provided by Company:
- All required PPE provided at no cost
- Training on proper use
- Regular inspection and replacement
- Custom fitting when needed

CHEMICAL & HAZARDOUS MATERIALS

Handling:
- Read Safety Data Sheets (SDS) before use
- Use in well-ventilated areas
- Wear appropriate PPE
- Store properly when not in use
- Dispose per regulations
- Report spills immediately

Storage:
- Locked cabinets for hazardous materials
- Proper labeling required
- Incompatible materials separated
- Regular inventory and inspection
- SDS binders in each area

VEHICLE SAFETY

Parking Lot:
- Speed limit: 5 mph
- Yield to pedestrians
- Park in designated spaces only
- Lock vehicle and remove valuables
- Report suspicious activity to Security

Company Vehicles:
- Valid driver's license required
- Pre-trip inspection mandatory
- No cell phone use while driving
- Seat belts required
- Report accidents immediately
- Maintain cleanliness

SECURITY PROCEDURES

Building Access:
- Badge required at all times
- Do not tailgate or allow tailgating
- Report lost badges immediately
- Visitor sign-in required
- Escort visitors at all times
- Lock offices when unattended

Data Security:
- Lock computer when away from desk
- Shred confidential documents
- Use secure file transfer for sensitive data
- Report suspicious emails to IT
- No unauthorized software
- Regular password changes required

TRAINING REQUIREMENTS

Mandatory Training:
- New hire safety orientation
- Annual safety refresher
- Fire drill participation (quarterly)
- Hazard communication (if applicable)
- Ergonomics training
- Emergency response procedures

Specialized Training:
- First aid/CPR (for designated responders)
- Forklift operation (warehouse only)
- Confined space entry (maintenance)
- Bloodborne pathogens (first responders)
- Hazmat handling (as needed)
